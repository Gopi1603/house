About This Research

Technical details about the model architecture

This research presents a hybrid deep learning approach for residential electricity consumption prediction using a CNN-BiLSTM-SA (Convolutional Neural Network - Bidirectional LSTM - Self-Attention) architecture.

CNN Layer

Extracts local temporal patterns and features from the input time series data.

BiLSTM Layer

Captures long-term temporal dependencies in both forward and backward directions.

Self-Attention

Focuses on the most relevant time steps for making accurate predictions.

Dataset

The model was trained on the UCI Individual Household Electric Power Consumption dataset, containing measurements from a household in Sceaux, France, collected over 4 years at 1-minute intervals.
